# Privacy Policy for Thrtie

**Last Updated: October 26, 2023**

At **Thrtie**, accessible from your mobile device, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Thrtie and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

## 1. Local Data Storage (Privacy First)

Thrtie is designed with a **privacy-first** philosophy. 

*   **No Cloud Storage:** All data you enter into the app—including income, expenses, transactions, and budget plans—is stored **locally on your device** using a secure internal database.
*   **No Accounts:** You are not required to create an account or provide an email address to use the app.
*   **No Data Harvesting:** We do not collect, sell, or share your financial data with third parties. Because your data never leaves your device, we (the developers) have no access to your personal financial information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Financial Data
We collect the data you explicitly provide to enable the app's functionality:
*   Budget categories and planned amounts.
*   Transaction details (amount, date, description, category).
*   Starting balances for monthly budgets.

### B. App Preferences
We store your settings locally, such as:
*   Preferred currency symbol.
*   Language selection.
*   App theme settings.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The information stored on your device is used solely to:
*   Calculate and display your monthly budget progress.
*   Generate reports and spending trends.
*   Provide a personalized user interface based on your preferences.

## 4. Data Security

The security of your data is dependent on the security of your mobile device. We recommend using standard device security features (such as Passcode, FaceID, or Fingerprint) to prevent unauthorized access to your phone and the data contained within Thrtie.

## 5. Third-Party Services

Thrtie does not use any third-party analytics or data-tracking services. We do not use advertising networks that track your behavior.

## 6. Backup and Portability

As Thrtie stores data locally, if you delete the app or lose your device, your data will be permanently lost unless you have a full device backup (e.g., via iCloud or Google Backup).

## 7. Children's Information

Thrtie does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. Since all data is stored locally, parents can manage their child's use of the app by managing the device itself.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
